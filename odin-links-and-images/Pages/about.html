About Me

Self-Taught Full Stack Developer

Hello and welcome! I’m a passionate, self-taught full-stack developer on a transformative journey to master the world of web development.

Over the past year, I’ve dedicated myself to studying programming through The Odin Project, a robust open-source curriculum that has challenged me and shaped my skills from the ground up. With every HTML tag, JavaScript function, and backend server I’ve built, I’ve fallen more in love with the creative and logical sides of coding.

Before transitioning into tech, I built my career in other professional fields—learning to lead, communicate, and solve real-world problems. These experiences gave me a strong foundation in adaptability, focus, and persistence—all of which I now bring into my development work.

I’m diving deep into full-stack development, specializing in modern technologies like JavaScript, Node.js, React, and more. I believe in clean code, continual learning, and building applications that make people’s lives easier or more enjoyable.

Thanks for stopping by—I’m excited to grow, collaborate, and contribute meaningfully to the tech world. Let’s build something great together.
